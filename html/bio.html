Career Background

Born and raised in Philadelphia, PA, I learned from an early age that I had a love for technology and music. This led me initially to find a career in the music industry, specifically in sound engineering.

I studied at The Los Angeles Recording School and during this time I was able to work with many different musicians helping them capture their performances as perfectly as possible!

While I was not meant for the music industry forever, the exposure to technology and especially problem solving eventually led me to pursue a career as a software engineer. I was immediately drawn to the satisfaction of solving a problem with code. I also love that there is not just one way to do things, but many ways. Music taught me to be creative, and coding has taught me that creativity is the name of the game.

I am extremely excited to start my career as a developer! I cannot wait to start working with a team and helping build the tools that make our world go round.

Personal Life

When not working on a project or learning new skills, I love spending time with my wife and daughter.
